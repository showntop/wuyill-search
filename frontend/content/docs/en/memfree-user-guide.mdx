## Wuyill Search

### The Use cases for each AI model

-   **GPT-4o Mini**: Streaming response. Lightweight tasks, such as summarizing text, translation, extracting text from images, etc.

-   **GPT-4o**: Streaming response. Most complex tasks, such as complex writing tasks, advanced content creation and research assistance, etc.

-   **O1-Mini**: Slow. Reasoning model. Especially good at coding, math and science.

-   **O1-Preview**: Slow. Reasoning model. The upgraded version of O1-Mini, can solve complex problems across domains.

-   **Claude 3.5 Sonnet**: Streaming response. As good at coding as O1-Mini.
